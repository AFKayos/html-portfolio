Best Modern Horror Movies

My top 3 recommended movies so far.

Barbarian (2022)

American horror thriller film written and directed by Zach Cregger. Beautiful cinematography, beautifully written that keeps you invest throughout. Worth the watch!

Get Out (2017)

American psychological horror film written, co-produced, and directed by Jordan Peele. A point of view not really explored in the horror genre and a great social commentary on the black experience in North America. A must see!

The Babadook (2014)

Australian psychological horror film written and directed by Jennifer Kent. A complex relationship between a mother and a children's book. Will have you hiding under the covers!
